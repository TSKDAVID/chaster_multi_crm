Chaster Client Support Policy (Test Document)

Company: Northwind Home Electronics
Version: 2026.04
Owner: Support Operations Team

1. Business Hours
- Live chat support: Monday-Friday, 09:00-18:00 local time.
- Email support: 24/7 intake, responses during business hours.
- Weekend coverage: urgent billing and account lockout issues only.

2. First Response Targets
- Chat: under 2 minutes.
- Email: under 30 minutes for priority customers, under 4 hours standard.
- Escalated technical cases: acknowledge within 15 minutes.

3. Order and Shipping FAQ
Q: How long does shipping take?
A: Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping takes 1-2 business days.

Q: How do customers track orders?
A: A tracking link is sent by email once the order is dispatched. Agents can also copy tracking from the order panel.

Q: Do we support international shipping?
A: Yes, to selected regions. Typical delivery window is 7-12 business days.

4. Returns and Refunds
- Return window: 14 days from delivery for unopened items.
- Opened items are eligible only for manufacturer defects.
- Refund processing: 5-10 business days after warehouse confirmation.
- Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless item arrived damaged.

5. Billing and Subscriptions
Q: How do customers cancel subscription?
A: Customers can cancel in account settings. Access remains until end of billing cycle.

Q: Can invoices be reissued?
A: Yes, agents can reissue invoices with updated legal entity details after verification.

Q: What verification is required for billing changes?
A: Confirm account email, last invoice ID, and company name on file.

6. Account Security
- Password reset requires email verification.
- If a user reports account takeover, lock account immediately and escalate to Security.
- Two-factor authentication should be recommended for all admin users.

7. Escalation Rules
Escalate to Tier 2 when:
- issue impacts payment processing
- repeated failure after 2 guided troubleshooting attempts
- customer reports data mismatch across devices

Escalate to Engineering when:
- issue is reproducible in latest production build
- severity is SEV1 (system down) or SEV2 (critical function blocked)
- logs show API failures not resolved by retry

8. Communication Guidelines
- Start with empathy and clear acknowledgement.
- Confirm customer goal in one sentence.
- Provide step-by-step actions in numbered format.
- End with confirmation request: “Did this solve your issue?”

9. Case Closure Checklist
- Root cause recorded.
- Resolution steps documented.
- Customer confirmation captured.
- Follow-up reminder scheduled if needed.
